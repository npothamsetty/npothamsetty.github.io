Hooray, you’ve found my blog! It’s still pretty new, so I’m still playing around with the structure and organization of my ideas. Rather than have a traditional “blog” structure, I’ve taken inspiration from non-linear site organization (like Andy Matuschak's), and figure that this site will evolve and grow as I contribute more to it. Before this, I’ve mostly explored topics like social determinants of health, systems theory, epistemic limits, and biases in the privacy of my own journal. I’m hoping to use this site as a way to better understand and connect my ideas, in addition to finding links to others' work that interests me.
